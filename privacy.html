Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 February 2025

This website provides a simple tool for generating printable times-table worksheets. We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how your information is handled.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data.

We do not collect:

  • Names or contact details
  • Account information (no accounts exist)
  • Cookies or tracking data
  • Analytics or usage statistics
  • IP addresses beyond standard server logs used by the hosting provider

2. How Your Information Is Used

Because we do not collect any personal information, no data is used, analysed, shared, or sold.

All worksheet generation takes place entirely within your device's browser.

3. Third-Party Services

This website does not use:

  • Google Analytics
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media integrations
  • External data processors

No personal data is shared with any third party.

4. Security

As no data is collected or stored, no user-specific security risks apply.

Our hosting provider may maintain basic server logs for security and maintenance.

5. Children's Privacy

This site is suitable for children and use in schools.

It does not collect any personal data and contains no advertising or tracking technologies.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect improvements to the website or legal requirements.

Any changes will be posted on this page.
