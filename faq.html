Frequently Asked Questions

What is this tool?

It is a free generator that creates printable times table worksheets instantly.

Is it really free?

Yes, completely free. No signup or login needed.

Who is it for?

Teachers, parents, tutors, and students who need quick multiplication practice sheets.

What formats do the worksheets use?

They generate clean printable PDF-style layouts directly from the browser.

Do you store any data?

No, nothing is stored. Everything is generated locally in your browser.
